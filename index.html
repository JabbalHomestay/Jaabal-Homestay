About Tirthan Jaabal Homestay

Tirthan Jaabal Homestay – Your River Side Retreat in Tirthan Valley Nestled on the serene banks of the Tirthan River, Tirthan Jaabal Homestay offers you a peaceful escape into the heart of nature. Surrounded by the lush greenery of Tirthan Valley, our homestay is perfect for those looking to unwind and reconnect with nature. It is near the Great Himalayan Nation Park (World Heritage Site). If you are looking for the Best Riverside Homestay in Tirthan Valley, you are in the right place.

Key Features:

Riverside Location: Wake up to the soothing sound of the flowing Tirthan River and enjoy the stunning views of the Tirthan Valley. Our Homestay, located beside the Tirthan River, makes us the Best river-touch property in Himachal.

Comfortable Rooms: We have 5 Cozy, well-furnished rooms with Tirthan River View, and the Interior ensuring a comfortable stay.

Local Cuisine: Savor the authentic flavors of Himachal Pradesh with our home-cooked meals.

Adventure Awaits: Explore trekking trails To the Famous Great Himalayan National Park, and Enjoy Trout fishing spots.

Bonfire & Stargazing: Spend your evenings around a warm bonfire, under a blanket of stars. Whether you're seeking adventure or a peaceful retreat, Tirthan Jaabal Homestay is your perfect getaway in Tirthan Valley.
